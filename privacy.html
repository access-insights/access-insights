Privacy

Privacy Policy.

Last updated: June 18, 2026

Access Insights LLC collects and uses personal data to operate its website, communicate with people who contact us, support research and advisory work, and provide internal operations support. We aim to collect only the information needed for those purposes and to handle it with care.

Information We Collect

Depending on how you interact with Access Insights, we may collect:

  • Contact details, such as name, email address, organization, role, and phone number.
  • Messages, inquiries, form submissions, preferences, consent records, and related communication history.
  • Research, project, or participation information that authorized participants, collaborators, contractors, advisors, or team members choose to provide.
  • Basic website and technical information, such as pages visited, browser type, device information, and security or diagnostic logs.

How We Use Information

Access Insights uses personal data to respond to inquiries, coordinate projects, manage research and advisory work, support authorized team workflows, maintain records of consent and preferences, improve our services, protect our systems, and meet legal or operational obligations.

SMS/Text Messaging

Access Insights may collect mobile phone numbers and SMS consent records from authorized employees, contractors, advisors, and approved team members who choose to use Rocky, the Access Insights AI operations assistant, by SMS. Access Insights uses this information only to provide internal operational messaging, including project coordination, meeting support, task updates, status requests, workflow notifications, and administrative support.

SMS consent is optional and is not required as a condition of working with Access Insights, participating in Access Insights projects, or using non-SMS Access Insights services.

Access Insights does not sell, rent, or share SMS opt-in data, mobile numbers, or text messaging consent with third parties for their marketing or promotional purposes.

Users may opt out of SMS messages at any time by replying STOP. Users may request help by replying HELP.

How We Store and Protect Information

Access Insights uses administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards intended to protect personal data against unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or disclosure. Access is limited to people and service providers who need the information for authorized business, research, operational, security, or compliance purposes.

No method of transmission or storage is perfectly secure. We review our practices as our systems and work evolve.

When We Share Information

Access Insights may share personal data with service providers that help operate the website, forms, communications, hosting, security, analytics, business administration, or internal operations. These providers are expected to use the information only to provide services to Access Insights.

We may also share information when required by law, to protect rights and safety, or with a person's direction or consent. Access Insights does not sell personal data.

Your Choices

You may choose what information to provide, decline optional communications, request updates to your information, or ask questions about privacy practices. SMS users may opt out by replying STOP and may request help by replying HELP.

To make a privacy request, contact Access Insights through the website contact form or through your established Access Insights project contact.

Changes to This Policy

Access Insights may update this privacy policy as our website, services, research operations, and internal systems change. The updated date above reflects the latest published version.
