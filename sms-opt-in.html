SMS consent

Access Insights Rocky SMS Opt-In.

Access Insights uses Rocky, an internal AI operations assistant, to support authorized employees, contractors, advisors, and approved team members with internal business operations.

Users may choose to receive SMS/text messages from Access Insights Rocky for project coordination, meeting support, task updates, status requests, workflow notifications, and administrative support.

SMS consent is optional and is not required as a condition of working with Access Insights, participating in Access Insights projects, or using non-SMS Access Insights services.

Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP to opt out or HELP for help.
