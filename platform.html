Infrastructure

More than a recruiting pool or survey tool.

The platform is designed to make human-centered research repeatable, ethical, and useful across projects. It supports participant readiness, partner onboarding, consent, communication preferences, privacy workflows, surveys, project operations, reporting, and future knowledge systems.
