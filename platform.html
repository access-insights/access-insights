Platform

A pipeline for turning unmet needs into scaled impact.

Access Insights identifies and scores unmet needs, pairs the strongest needs with solution candidates, manages the research needed to validate them, and activates partners who can help solutions reach the people who need them most.

Industry gap

The right problem matters as much as the right prototype.

Innovation pipelines often break at the front end and the back end. Teams may build around an assumed need without enough evidence about consumer barriers, lived context, or the scale of the opportunity. Even strong prototypes can stall if they are not connected to partners who can deploy, support, distribute, or sustain them.

Access Insights addresses both gaps through research innovation infrastructure: a disciplined way to prioritize unmet needs, shape research opportunities, validate solution qualities, and connect promising work to collaborators positioned for scaled impact.

Pipeline execution

From signals to scale.

The platform turns human variability into structured research priorities, then carries the strongest opportunities through validation and partner activation.

  1. Capture unmet need signals. Gather barriers, consumer needs, lived-context patterns, and opportunity signals from participants, partners, projects, and field work.
  2. Assess scale of impact. Pair human need with diligence on affected populations, market opportunity, operational feasibility, and potential reach.
  3. Score needs against a rubric. Use predefined criteria to compare unmet needs and bring forward the opportunities most likely to matter.
  4. Pair needs with solution candidates. Connect high-scoring needs to product, service, policy, or infrastructure ideas that could plausibly address them.
  5. Create research opportunities. Turn the need-plus-solution pairing into a research opportunity: the concept from which platform research projects are scoped.
  6. Run platform-managed research. Coordinate participant matching, consent, surveys, interviews, usability studies, Living Labs activity, synthesis, and reporting.
  7. Validate solution qualities. Determine whether a solution is useful, usable, understandable, trusted, accessible, supportable, and ready for real-world conditions.
  8. Activate collaboration partners. Identify partners with the reach, expertise, delivery channels, sites, services, or institutional capacity to help high-impact solutions reach people.

Research contexts

Structured research across real-world settings.

Different questions require different research environments. The platform supports lightweight community surveys, focused interviews, usability studies, concept validation, field research, and Living Labs work where day-to-day context matters.

Participant-centered studies

Surveys, interviews, usability sessions, and concept reviews help identify needs, test assumptions, and understand how people evaluate potential solutions.

Partner-supported contexts

Partners can contribute domain expertise, access to relevant environments, referral pathways, operational perspective, and research capacity.

Living Labs

Living Labs are collaborations with independent living communities, assisted living communities, and similar settings where resident, staff, and leadership perspectives are included together.

Platform layers

Research infrastructure that compounds learning.

Each layer strengthens the full pipeline: what needs are surfaced, how research is run, what evidence is retained, and how insight is translated into action.

Need intelligence

Structured signals from lived experience, barriers, consumer needs, context, and impact potential.

Participant community

Trusted participants, research readiness, preferences, contribution history, and support paths.

Partner Network

Organizations, sites, referrals, expertise, delivery capacity, distribution, and strategic collaboration.

Research operations

Project intake, research workflows, surveys, interviews, artifacts, reporting, and coordination.

Governance

Consent, privacy, retention, deletion workflows, role boundaries, and auditability.

Data and insight systems

Data synthesis, reusable research assets, aggregate insight repositories, and AI-enhanced trend analysis.
