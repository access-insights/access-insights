Shaping a human-centered future.

Technology is changing faster than society can adapt. Access Insights is building research infrastructure that helps identify the highest-value human needs, generate trusted evidence, and connect promising solutions with the partners who can bring them to scale.

Strategic rationale

Better decisions create better innovation.

Technology is advancing faster than our ability to understand how people will use it, trust it, and adapt with it. Access Insights helps organizations focus on high-value opportunities before development begins, build trustworthy evidence, and move successful ideas toward lasting adoption.

Before development

Focus effort where value is highest

Use disability, aging, and human variability as powerful sources of insight into barriers, adaptation, and trust.

During development

Build evidence people can trust

Test whether ideas are useful, usable, accessible, trusted, and ready for everyday conditions.

Toward implementation

Connect validated work to scale

Help promising work reach the people and organizations that can turn evidence into lasting benefit.

Proto-Lab

Exploring what comes next.

Proto-Lab explores ideas that emerge from patterns we see across research, technology, and community partnerships. It is where we invest our own curiosity into tools and concepts intended to create shared benefit.

Open Source

UnisonOS

An operating surface for intention-centered computing.

Treats a person's intention as the primary interface, with voice, text, vision, touch, Braille, sign, and BCI as first-class channels. Accessibility is the model, not a mode.
