Virtual Lex

Establishing presence when you cannot be present.

Virtual Lex began as a way for Lex Frieden to virtually attend a series of 35th anniversary celebrations with a realistic sense of being in the room.

  • High-precision binaural audio and cameras support seeing, hearing, and interacting with the audience.
  • A physical torso with glasses-mounted camera and binaural microphones in the ears enables the remote presence experience.
  • The torso can turn toward people asking questions for direct engagement.
  • Audience members experience Lex as an avatar with animation and lip-sync matched to his actual speech.
