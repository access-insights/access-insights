Proto-Lab

Builds from the needs research reveals.

Proto-Lab is a space for tools, projects, and resources worth finding: ones that address real human needs but sit outside our current pipeline work. Some emerged from our own research; others were built independently. We keep publishing them as we find them.

Open Source

UnisonOS

An operating surface for intention-centered computing.

Instead of asking people to navigate apps, files, and windows, UnisonOS treats intention as the primary interface and orchestrates services, agents, context, and modalities around the person. Voice, text, vision, touch, Braille, sign, and BCI are first-class channels; accessibility is the model, not a mode.

Virtual Lex

Establishing presence when you cannot be present.

A physical torso uses glasses-mounted cameras and binaural microphones positioned at the ears, and can turn toward people asking questions so the remote participant can hear, see, and engage. People in the room experience an animated, lip-synced avatar: a visible social presence, not a flat video feed.
