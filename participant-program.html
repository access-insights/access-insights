Participant Program

Share your experience. Help shape what gets built.

Everyone is welcome in the Access Insights Participant Program. The community represents the full spectrum of human experience and gives people a clear, accessible, consent-based way to contribute to research.

Join the participant programLearn how it works

How participation works.

Who this is for

Everyone is welcome, with explicit encouragement for people with disabilities, aging adults, caregivers, family members, support professionals, and people navigating access or service barriers.

Ways to contribute

Readiness profiles, surveys, interviews, usability studies, co-design sessions, referrals, and future advisory opportunities.

What you receive

Relevant opportunities, less repeated intake, communication and accessibility preferences, consent visibility, support paths, and possible compensation when projects include it.

Trust and choice

Your participation is voluntary.

Access Insights uses consent-based workflows and does not sell personal participant data. You can set preferences, update your profile, and use support or privacy request paths when needed.
