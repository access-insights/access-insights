User Experience Program

Share your experience. Help shape what gets built.

Everyone is welcome in the Access Insights User Experience Program. The community represents the full spectrum of human experience and gives people a clear, accessible, consent-based way to contribute to research.

Join the user experience programLearn how it works

How participation works.

Who this is for

Everyone is welcome, with explicit encouragement for people with disabilities, aging adults, caregivers, family members, support professionals, and people navigating access or service barriers. A growing community has more power to be heard.

Ways to contribute

  • Register for the program.
  • Provide your demographic details to be eligible for matching to compensated research projects.
  • Share your perspective through community surveys.
  • Refer others to join the community.

What you receive

  • Potential to earn compensation through research participation.
  • Visibility into quantitative research insights for projects and surveys you contribute to.
  • Accrue contribution points to achieve higher participant levels.

Participant levels

Recognition for continued engagement.

Program levels recognize engagement over time. Contributions can include completing readiness items, participating in research activities, completing surveys, and referring new community members.

Level 1: Program Member

Joined the User Experience Program and started the profile process.

Level 2: Research Ready Member

Completed readiness details that support matching to relevant research opportunities.

Level 3: Insight Contributor

Completed contribution-weighted activities such as surveys or research sessions.

Level 4: Community Builder

Contributes across activities and helps grow the community through referrals.

Level 5: Strategic Advisor

Sustained engagement may qualify members to help shape program direction.

Trust and choice

Your participation is voluntary.

Access Insights uses consent-based workflows and does not sell personal participant data. You can set preferences, update your profile, and use support or privacy request paths when needed.
