# Phase 3 Page-Level Messaging

Last updated: 2026-06-13

## Direction

Position Access Insights as research innovation infrastructure for the full spectrum of human experience. The message should emphasize an end-to-end pipeline: identify the right unmet needs, evaluate scale of impact, create research opportunities, validate solution qualities through the platform, and activate partners who can help solutions reach real communities.

The site should not explicitly ask for funding. It should implicitly appeal to mission-aligned funders by showing disciplined prioritization, credible research operations, governance, participant trust, partner activation, and a model for scaled impact.

## Home

Hero:

- H1: Technology is moving faster than ever. Human experience is not.
- Support: Access Insights builds research innovation infrastructure to identify high-impact needs, validate solutions, and accelerate the path from insight to impact.

Key section messages:

- Products fail when teams design for averages, chase the wrong problems, or stop at prototypes that never reach the people who need them.
- Programs create two pathways to meaningful impact: user experience contributors surface lived experience and unmet needs; partners contribute expertise, capacity, and reach.
- The pipeline model moves from unmet need to scaled impact by identifying the right problems, scoring needs, creating research opportunities, validating solution qualities, and activating partners.

## Platform

Hero:

- H1: A pipeline for turning unmet needs into scaled impact.
- Support: Access Insights identifies and scores unmet needs, pairs the strongest needs with solution candidates, manages the research needed to validate them, and activates partners who can help solutions reach the people who need them most.

Core pipeline:

1. Capture unmet need signals.
2. Assess scale of impact.
3. Score needs against a rubric.
4. Pair needs with solution candidates.
5. Create research opportunities.
6. Run platform-managed research.
7. Validate solution qualities.
8. Activate collaboration partners.

Value drivers:

- Better problem selection.
- Evidence-backed prioritization.
- Representative research grounded in human variability.
- Clear consent, privacy, and governance.
- Partner activation for real-world reach.
- Reusable knowledge systems over time.

## Programs

Message:

- Better research needs broader human experience and stronger delivery networks.
- Participants help surface barriers, needs, opportunities, and design realities.
- Partners help turn validated opportunities into practical paths for adoption, deployment, support, and scale.

Primary paths:

- User Experience Program: `research.accessinsights.net`
- Partner Network: `partner.accessinsights.net`

## Lab & Insights

Message:

- Access Insights turns research priorities into projects, prototypes, open community tools, and public thinking.
- Articles & Insights can later integrate a Substack or newsletter feed when that channel exists.
