# Phase 2 Sitemap And Content Architecture

Last updated: 2026-06-13

## Direction

The Access Insights site should present a focused platform/program model. The center of gravity is research innovation infrastructure: a disciplined pipeline for identifying high-impact unmet needs, validating solution qualities through ethical user research, and activating partners who can help promising solutions reach the people who need them most.

The site should not directly ask for funding. It should be attractive to mission-aligned funders through credibility, governance, prioritization discipline, visible projects, and the ability to execute across the full innovation pipeline. It should also leave room for paid platform collaboration without making paid engagement conversion the main organizing principle.

Human variability is the core frame. Disability, accessibility, and aging remain central strengths because they reveal important design realities, but Access Insights should not be positioned as a disability-only solution.

### Platform: `/platform.html`

Job: explain the end-to-end research pipeline.

Core sections:

- Hero: a pipeline for turning unmet needs into scaled impact.
- Industry gap: innovation pipelines often break at the front end and back end.
- Pipeline execution: signals, scale assessment, need scoring, solution candidates, research opportunities, platform-managed research, solution-quality validation, partner activation.
- Platform layers: need intelligence, participant community, partner network, research operations, governance, and knowledge systems.
- Trust model and research platform link.
